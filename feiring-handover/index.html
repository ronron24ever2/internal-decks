Executive summary

Letter of Intent is signed; the operational and KPI groundwork is not — ops inherits both

Three things to internalise before reading the rest of this document. Everything below this slide elaborates one of these.

01
Commercial frame is locked. Letter of Intent v4 signed 28 April 2026 by Roar Aaserud (Maskinsjef, Feiring) and Ronny Liverød (VP Heavy Machinery, Hive Autonomy Operations AS). Three-phase structure: free pilot on one Hive-provided Volvo L180 (Phase 1), then commercial operation on two Feiring machines (Phase 2), then on-site operations centre (Phase 3). Both parties can exit Phase 1 with two weeks' written notice. Norwegian law, venue Agder.
02
Start date slips from week 20 to week 23. The Letter of Intent reads "mid-May 2026". That is no longer realistic. Severin is bound to closing Yara through 28 May. Two weeks' move from week 20 to week 23 (2–6 June) is the minimum honest re-baseline. Roar has not been told yet.
03
Concrete acceptance KPIs do not exist yet. The contract names five framework criteria (uptime, loading precision, safety, operator experience, cost basis for Phase 2). Thresholds and measurement method must be agreed within three weeks of pilot start and signed as an addendum (§ 2.3). Owner is not assigned. This is ops' first deliverable.

Phase 1 is fully specified and signed; Phase 2 and Phase 3 are intent only, deferred to separate agreements

The Letter of Intent regulates Phase 1 in full and establishes intent toward Phases 2 and 3. Commercial terms for Phase 2 are not negotiated; do not assume them.

Phase 1 — pilot
Free validation, one Hive-provided L180

Hive runs one test machine on Feiring sites to prove acceptance criteria under real operation. No invoicing between parties. Either side may terminate with two weeks' written notice.

Status: signed, not started
Phase 2 — operations
Remote operation, two Feiring-owned machines

Hive installs the solution on two of Feiring's own wheel loaders and operates them remotely from Kristiansand. Pricing, install cost, runtime, duration, service boundary — all to be negotiated in separate Term Sheet / MSA.

Status: intent only — no commercials
Phase 3 — on-site centre
Operations centre at Feiring, Feiring operators

When autonomy maturity allows: one operator supervises 4–6 machines from a Feiring-staffed control room, after Hive training. Scope, investment, operating model in separate agreement.
